Thems Trails

Welcome to Them Trails! We are a community of trail runners who love to explore the great outdoors. Whether you're a seasoned trail runner or just starting out, we have something for everyone. Join us for group runs two times a week or join us on one of our many events throughout the year. We are a LBGTQIQA + and allies looking to offer a safe community of runners who enjoy trail running. So lace up your shoes, grab your hydration pack, and let's hit the trails together!
